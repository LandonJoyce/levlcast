Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

LevlCast ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LevlCast mobile application and website (levlcast.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account information: When you sign in with Twitch or Apple, we receive your display name, username, email address, and profile picture.
  • Stream data: We access your Twitch VODs and follower count via the Twitch API to provide coaching reports and growth analytics.
  • Usage data: We track the number of VOD analyses and clips generated to enforce plan limits.
  • Purchase information: Subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of your subscription status but never your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered stream coaching reports from your VOD content
  • To display growth analytics based on your follower history
  • To enforce free and Pro plan usage limits
  • To improve our AI coaching models and platform features

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase infrastructure with row-level security. Authentication tokens are stored encrypted on your device using iOS Secure Enclave. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Twitch: Used for authentication and accessing your VODs and follower data. Subject to Twitch's Privacy Notice.
  • Apple: Used for Sign in with Apple and subscription processing. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • Anthropic Claude: Your VOD transcripts are sent to Anthropic's API to generate coaching reports. No personally identifiable information is included beyond stream content.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage subscription state. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at mototoka14@gmail.com.

6. Children's Privacy

LevlCast is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at mototoka14@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at mototoka14@gmail.com.
